📚 BLAHAJ FACTS YOU DIDN'T ASK FOR 📚

  • Blåhaj (pronounced "BLAW-high") means "blue shark" in Swedish. It's basically a name that just means what it is. Swedish honesty. Respect.
  • Blahaj is 55 cm (~22 inches) long and costs around $25 USD. Best money you'll ever spend. Fight me.
  • It became a beloved trans community symbol because of its soft, comforting vibe and its IKEA blue-and-white colouring — and because the trans community has impeccable taste.
  • Blahaj has been to space (in tweets, memes, and our hearts). No we won't elaborate.
  • There are entire subreddits, Discord servers, and Tumblr blogs dedicated entirely to people photographing their blåhaj in increasingly unhinged situations. This website is one of them.
  • Blahaj pairs well with: sensory needs, anxiety, late-night coding sessions, gender feelings, and any situation where you need a soft shark immediately.
  • IKEA briefly discontinued Blahaj in some regions and the internet had a collective breakdown. It was restored. The people demanded it. Democracy works sometimes.
  • Scientists have confirmed* that hugging a Blahaj releases serotonin, dopamine, and pure shark energy. (*scientists here means "people on the internet" but the vibes are correct)
